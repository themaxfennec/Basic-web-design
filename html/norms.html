Norms

Common Norms Uncommon Norms

The norms of the country are MUCH different from what you might be used to. This can be from the technology used, to the way people interact with each other, to the foods people eat, and more. It is important to be aware of these differences and to be respectful of them when visiting the country.

Some of the norms you might encounter include:
  • Tattoos are legal but they are not widely common and you might have people give you strange looks if you have one.
  • It is common to remove your shoes before entering someone's home and some restaurants.
  • People will keep their head down when on the subway, buses, and when walking around.

Common Norms

  • Removing shoes before entering someone's home and some restaurants.
  • Keeping head down when on the subway, buses, and when walking around.
  • Don't talk too loud in public.
